TalentBridge BI System
This is a 14-table relational database business intelligence prototype designed for recruitment and placement auditing.

Key Features:
Penetration Funnel: Cross-table tracking from lead to placement.

Compliance Audit: Automatic flagging of placements without interview records.

Financial Integrity: 7-attribute tracking for overdue and high-risk debt (Monthly & Full Upfront).

Retention Analytics: Dynamic cohort retention tracking based on onboarding logs.
